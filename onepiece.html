Overview

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the power to stretch like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. He sails the Grand Line with his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, in search of the legendary treasure known as the One Piece, hoping to become the King of the Pirates.

  • Adventure and Perseverance
